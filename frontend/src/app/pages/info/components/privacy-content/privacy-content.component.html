1. Introduction & Data Protection Officer

This Privacy Policy explains how LegalConnect (referred to as "we", "us", or "the Platform") collects, processes, and protects personal data belonging to users, legal professionals, and visitors (collectively referred to as Data Principals under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023).

For grievances, data principal right requests, or questions concerning data processing, you can contact our designated Data Protection Officer (DPO) at: dpo@legalconnect.com.

2. Categories of Personal Data Collected

We process personal data solely based on your explicit consent, which is collectable during signup, form submissions, or when using cookie preferences. The categories of data processed include:

Account Identity Information

Full Name, Email Address, Phone Number, User Role, and Password hash (encrypted via BCrypt).

Advocate Credentials

Bar Council registration ID, practice specializations, experience years, office address, and education details.

Interaction Data

Client consultation messages, reviews, star ratings, and bookmarked Bare Act sections.

Technical Logs

IP Address, browser User-Agent, cookie preference audit trail, and secure login histories.

3. Purpose of Processing

Under Section 4 of India's DPDP Act 2023, we only process personal data for specific, lawful purposes as a Data Fiduciary:

  • Providing services: Authenticating user sessions, saving research bookmarks, and routing case templates.
  • Consultation dispatch: Securing the transmission of your legal query details directly to your chosen advocate's dashboard.
  • Directory analytics: Compiling profile views and average response speed metric logs to evaluate advocate engagement metrics (if analytics consent is granted).
  • Platform notification dispatch: Disseminating email notifications for system releases or legislative changes.

4. Rights of the Data Principal

As a Data Principal under Section 11-14 of the DPDP Act 2023, you hold the following rights which can be requested by emailing the DPO or triggering the automated request dashboard below:

  • Right to Summary: You can request a summary of your personal data currently processed by us.
  • Right to Correction & Erasure: You can edit your profile details directly from the settings page, or request deletion of your entire account and associated bookmarks/reviews.
  • Right of Grievance Redressal: You can file disputes regarding data handling directly with our DPO or escalate to the Data Protection Board of India.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent through our Cookie Preferences center or register a grievance using an authorized Consent Manager.

5. Data Sharing & Third-Parties

LegalConnect will never sell, rent, or lease your personal data. We only share information with third-party processors necessary for platform operations, specifically:

  • Lawyers: Your query details and name are shared with the advocate you explicitly choose to contact.
  • SendGrid API: Email addresses and message bodies are routed to SendGrid servers strictly for transactional email delivery.
  • Infrastructure Hosting: Firebase and GCP (Google Cloud Platform) secure databases where data remains encrypted.

6. Data Security & Retention

All personal data is encrypted at rest and in transit (via SSL/TLS). In compliance with legal mandates, we retain personal data only for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your profile or request erasure, your details are wiped from active databases within 30 days, except where retention is required by Indian statutory obligations (e.g. tax, audit logs).
