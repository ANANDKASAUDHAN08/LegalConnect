Lok Adalat & Free Legal Aid Options

This type of dispute is highly eligible for resolution in Lok Adalat (Peoples Court) or through free legal aid. Under Section 12 of the Legal Services Authorities Act, all women, children, and citizens with annual income below slabs (varies from Rs. 1 Lakh to 3 Lakhs depending on state) are entitled to free legal aid lawyers and complete refund of court fees.

Women, children, and low-income citizens are entitled to free legal aid and complete court fee refunds under Sec 12 of the LSA Act. Disputes are also highly eligible for fast-track resolution in Lok Adalat.
